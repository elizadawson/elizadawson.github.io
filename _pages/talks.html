"Predicting the future of Antarctica and Greenland starts with characterizing what is happening deep beneath the ice surface."
Conditions at the ice sheet base govern ice flow and stability, yet they remain poorly understood and underrepresented in models. I work at the intersection of computational geophysics, numerical modeling, and climate science to close this gap, improving predictions of ice sheet change in a warming world.
