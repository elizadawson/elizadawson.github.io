"Predicting the future of polar ice sheets and global sea level requires connecting what we can observe today with how ice will evolve tomorrow."
My work builds new ways to turn geophysical signals into physical understanding, from radar echoes deep inside ice to models that project ice loss and sea-level rise under a changing climate."
